Guthrie Chamber of Commerce - Site Plan

Guthrie Chamber of Commerce

This name represents the the town that the chamber will be operating in.

Site Purpose

The Guthrie Chamber of Commerce acts as a binding entity in the community. It helps to promote businesses and bring forth growth for both the community and the economic situation in the area. This site will help to communicate with the members of both the chamber and the community about the businesses in the area and any events that they will be hosting.

Scenarios

  • What events will be held each month to promote networking within the community and chamber members.
  • How has the chamber impacted the economic growth in the area.
  • How has the chamber impacted the population growth in the area by bringing in new businesses and jobs.

Color Schema

Navy (#000080) - used for headers and navigation.

Green (#5b945b) - used for accents and buttons.

White (#ffffff) - used for any text that is in the Navy Blue areas
